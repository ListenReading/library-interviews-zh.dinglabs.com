When you are a director, not only do you have a responsibility to your investors, you also have a responsibility to your employees. Because when building up your business, it’s your employees that are going to help you. If you seek to make your employees happy and content in their positions, it will be easier for them to do their work and build your business. The second thing (you must do) is be responsible for your clients. If we do business for a long time and our clients are satisfied then they will want to stick with us for the long term. Also, we have to take responsibility for the environment. This is because the environment is wholly connected and all parts influence one another. If we do business and do not consider the environment, in the short term the benefits may be good, but in the long run it is going to (adversely) influence our business and influence the profits of our investors. If we are in the food service industry, we must be very serious about the safety of our food products. You must spend a lot of money on training and educating your staff, and operate in accordance with government regulations. In this way (you can ensure) that your customers will not get food poisoning, etc. This is everyone’s responsibility…a social responsibility. Therefore, being a director or similar leadership position involves always handling these things very well. Then everyone wins and you can operate a more long-lasting business.
