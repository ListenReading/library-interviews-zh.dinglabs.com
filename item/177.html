I do not agree with this viewpoint. I feel that actually, it is not a question of what language you speak. I feel that in doing business person to person, the pivotal thing is to establish a kind of trust. So in actuality, this comes back to the basics of person to person relationships. Will the two of you be able to come to a meeting of the minds? Will you be able to connect in your thinking and ideas? Can you respect and come to understand each other? I feel that this is the true language of business, rather than the language of this country or the local dialect.
