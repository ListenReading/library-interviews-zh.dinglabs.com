189: Hong Shi - Professional - Asking for Information
China, Xiamen
< PreviousIndexNext >
In China, if you need someone’s help, need information from someone, the person will basically tell you everything he knows. If he doesn’t know, he’ll act very responsibly by telling you that he doesn’t know. Or he’ll at least give you a reliable lead. That is, he won’t give you a lead unless he believes that the lead he’s giving is 90% or more than 99% reliable. There are other times when you need to think about the person’s personality. If… some people are very enthusiastic about helping others, and they might even help you follow up on a lead. That is, they’ll look for the information for you themselves, then give it to you. That’s basically how it is.在中国如果你有需要别人的帮助，从别人那里获得信息，那么基本上这个人会告诉你他所知道的。如果他不知道的话，他会很有责任心的告诉你他不知道。或者至少他会给你一个比较有可信度的线索。也就是说他相信他给的这个线索有百分之九十或九十九以上的可信度，他才会就是给你这个线索。还有一些呢就说要看看个人的这个性格了，如果有一些人非常热心于帮助别人的话，那么他会甚至于帮你去找这个线索。就是他们自己帮你找这个这个信息，然后再提供给你。啊基本上是这样吧。
