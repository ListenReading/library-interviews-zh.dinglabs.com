142: Alexander Ho - Professional - Needs of Investors vs. Society
Taiwan, (Raised in Hawaii)

The senior director of a company feels (the effects of) a lot of different responsibilities. The first is of course to the investors. Another is to society. He also has a social responsibility. For example, in the case of environmental protection and other such aspects this has been my experience. When a company is starting out, it is much like a newly formed nation. The most basic and fundamental needs must be fulfilled. For example, a nation can’t think about environmental protection if its people don’t have food to eat, a place to sleep, good water to drink, etc. Only after these can you start thinking about the next step…higher living standards/habits, etc. At present, the Chinese government has already approved the spending of 1.75 billion U.S. dollars for environmental protection plans. This shows that they have already reached this level (of development). If a company reaches this level and if they can give their investors some positive returns, only then can they start thinking about social and environmental service and responsibility.
