81: Chao Robert Zheng - Negotiation - Reacting to Initial Resistance
China, Hunan

Um, I think, this sort of resistance is a very natural phenomenon, not only between Americans and Chinese, but between any two groups of differing peoples. Because you have so many areas in which you are different, this is a very natural reaction. However, I feel that when this happens, we (those involved) are the only ones who have the ability to gradually open our hearts/minds and make a genuine effort, from the heart, to accept and learn from each other. I feel this is simply a person to person process whereby we establish relationships. So (I think) this process can be completed very quickly. After that, we can quickly get into the real topic…get into the negotiations.嗯，我想，这个阻力的话哈，是一个很自然的一个现象，不光是在中国人和美国人 之间，在任何一个两个不同的民族之间，因为你们有这么多的不同的地方，所以是个自然的反映。但是，我觉得这个时候呢，只有我们真的是能把我们的心和慢慢能够敞开，用心去接纳，体验对方的话，我觉得这个只是一个人与人之间的，一个建立关系的过程。所以，这个过程能够，能够，很快就能够过去。然后，我们可以很快地进入正题，进行这个谈判。
